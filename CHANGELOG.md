## v1.3.0

* Added support for full PFB inversion
* Added options for toggling output formats:
  - **-i** incoherently summed PSRFITS
  - **-p** coherently summed PSRFITS
  - **-v** PFB inverted (IFFT method) VDIF
  - **-u** PFB inverted (full, 'Ord' method) VDIF
